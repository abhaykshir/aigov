# Governance

## Current maintainer

**Abhay Kshirsagar** ([@abhaykshir](https://github.com/abhaykshir)) — project creator and maintainer.

## Decision process

The maintainer decides on merges, architecture direction, and releases. Community input is welcomed and encouraged via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/abhaykshir/aigov/issues) and [Discussions](https://github.com/abhaykshir/aigov/discussions).

For significant changes — new frameworks, scanner architecture, breaking CLI changes — open an issue to discuss before submitting a PR. This keeps effort aligned and avoids wasted work.

## Evolution

As the community grows, governance will evolve — including adding co-maintainers and forming a steering committee for regulatory framework accuracy. If you are interested in taking on a more active role, open an issue.

## Regulatory accuracy

Classification rules are based on published regulatory text but are **not legal advice**. aigov helps engineering teams build an AI inventory and identify likely risk categories — it does not replace legal counsel. Users should consult qualified legal counsel for compliance decisions.

Regulatory frameworks evolve. If you identify an inaccuracy in the classification rules, please open an issue with a reference to the relevant article or guidance document.
